How to play?

Each participant attempts to beat the dealer by getting a count as close to 21 as possible, without going over 21.
If your initial hand is equal to 21, it is called a “blackjack” and you automatically win 3:2

Card values

- 10

- 1 or 11

Cards 2 up to 10 are the same value as their number.

Buttons

A hand can "hit" as many times as desired until the total is 21 or more. Players must stand on a total of 21.

To 'Stand' is to hold your total and end your turn.

To double your bet is permitted only on two-card hand totals of 9, 10, or 11.
